2020 SENIOR DH CHAMPION OF CATALONIA

The 2020 Honor Division team led by José Luis Riera is the current Campió de Catalunya 2020 and has qualified 4th in the Honor Division League with the greatest presence in its history of homegrown players trained in the lower categories.





CHILDREN CHAMPION OF SPAIN 2015

El equipo Infantil ha realizado un gran Campeonato venciendo en una semi final espectacular a Sant Boi por 1-0 y en la final a Villalbilla.




CHAMPIONS OF THE CUP OF THE KING 2014

The Barcelona Baseball Club has been proclaimed in Bilbao Champion of the Copa del Rey by beating the Marlins of Tenerife by 12-2 in 7 innings. Great game by Eric Paez from the mound and Oscar Angulo on offense being the MVP of the final, with a home run and a two-run single. In the semi-final, a great match for Larry Suárez against Sant Boi to get the victory by 6 to 3.




THE CADET CHAMPION OF SPAIN 2013

The Cadet team of the Barcelona Baseball Club has been proclaimed Champion of Spain 2014. Great Tournament of our cadets who have finished the competition undefeated and playing a high level baseball.
