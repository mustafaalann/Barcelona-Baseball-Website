LETTER FROM THE PRESIDENT

First of all, I want to welcome you and your family to our club.

Whether you are completely unfamiliar with our sport or have experience in it, our goal is to develop your skills so that you can become a good baseball player, but without forgetting basic aspects such as camaraderie and respect for others (coaches). , players of yours and other teams, referees, etc.).

My advice is that before you make the decision to join our club, make sure you really like baseball. And to do that, nothing better than coming to some

workouts and train with us. If you can also come and watch a game, much better.

With these pages we have tried to clarify a series of doubts, as much of schedule and organization as of the sport in himself, that habitually have the people who like you approach our section. If you need more information, do not hesitate to ask your team delegate.

I hope you enjoy the experience, and that you will soon be one of the members of our teams.

Cordially,
JOSEP JUANEDA
President
